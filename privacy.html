Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

At Modern Calculator, developed by Aykutech Dev., we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our application does not directly collect, store, or share any personal identity information (such as name, email, phone number, etc.). Modern Calculator does not require any registration or personal account to perform its core functions.

2. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

To provide our services for free, we display advertisements via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use anonymous identifiers to serve personalized ads and measure performance, including:

  • Advertising IDs (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (Model, OS version)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

For more details on how Google handles this data, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.

3. User Preferences

You can limit data collection for personalized ads by adjusting the settings on your device (e.g., "Reset Advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it from our records.
