Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

At Aykutech Dev., we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile applications, including Modern Calculator, MyLibrary, and Vakt-i Namaz.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our applications do not directly collect, store, or share any personal identity information (such as name, email, phone number, etc.). For MyLibrary and Vakt-i Namaz, all records, settings, and progress data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Camera and Media Permissions

The MyLibrary application requires access to your device's Camera and Photo Gallery. These permissions are used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to take a photo of a physical book cover.
  • To allow you to select an existing image from your gallery as a book cover.

Images captured or selected via these permissions remain on your device and are not collected by Aykutech Dev. Applications like Modern Calculator and Vakt-i Namaz do not require or use these permissions.

3. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

To provide our services for free, we display advertisements via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use anonymous identifiers to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising IDs (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (Model, OS version)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

For more details, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.

4. User Preferences

You can limit data collection for personalized ads by adjusting the settings on your device (e.g., "Reset Advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you discover that your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.
